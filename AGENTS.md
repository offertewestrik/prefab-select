# Prefab Select Design System & Guidelines

## Core Aesthetic
- **Style**: Modern, Premium, Architectural, Minimalist.
- **Vibe**: High-end architecture firm. Not just a builder, but "Artisans of Space".
- **Colors**: 
  - Primary: `blue-950` (Deep Navy)
  - Accent: `blue-600` (Branding Blue)
  - Highlights: `blue-400` (Light Blue)
  - Backgrounds: `white` or `blue-50/30`
- **Typography**: 
  - Headlines: Large, bold, uppercase, `tracking-tighter`, `leading-none`. Use italic/font-light accents for "human" touch.
  - Body: Clean, readable, generous line-height (`leading-relaxed`).

## Layout Patterns
- **Spacing**: Large padding/margins (`py-24` or `py-32`).
- **Cards**:
  - `rounded-[2.5rem]` or `rounded-[3rem]`.
  - Border: `border-slate-100`.
  - Shadow: Soft `shadow-sm`, hover `shadow-[0_40px_80px_rgba(29,78,216,0.1)]`.
  - Interaction: Hover lift (`-translate-y-2`) and subtle gradient glow.
- **Grids**: 3 columns on desktop, 1 on mobile.

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Large, rounded (`rounded-2xl`).
  - Font: `font-black uppercase tracking-widest text-[11px]`.
  - Primary: `bg-blue-600`, shadowed.
  - Secondary: `backdrop-blur-md border hover:bg-white/10`.
- **Hero Sections**: Full-width, high-quality architectural imagery, dark overlays for readability, centered or left-aligned typography.

## Principles
1. **Consistency**: Same styling for all pages (Diensten, About, Contact, etc.).
2. **Whitespace**: Be generous with empty space.
3. **Rust & Overzicht**: Keep layouts simple and quiet. Avoid clutter.
4. **Trust**: Use big numbers/stats and trust badges.
